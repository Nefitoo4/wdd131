1. Am I going to really save money if I start with this diet?

Definitely yes, you are going to save money if you decide to change your feeding. This page is not a training program and also a professional direction of nutrition, this page only provide ideas to start the way of austerity is up to you what would you like to eat and how to save more money in a safety way but we strongly recommend to follow these ideas to start a healthy and economic life.

2. Can I cook these meals in any country of the world?

Definitely, all these meals were thought to find the ingredients in any country in the world and these meals are easy to prepare and cheap.

3. How money can I save by following the meals of this page?

There is no amount or percentage of the saving, this would depend on how the inflation of the economy in each country, however there is no doubt of a reduction in your expenses. In our calculation have have an approximate of a 25% of savings however this is only an approximation and depends on the economy of each country and the value of each product.

4. Can a diabetic or renal failure patient follow the meals recipe?

No, the page was built for fans of health and economy wellness or any other beneficial contribution that this can bring.
